Natural Superfood

Tigernut
Ofio / Aya

Nigeria's ancient superfood — sweet, nutty, and extraordinarily nourishing

Tigernuts (known as Ofio in Yoruba and Aya in Hausa) are tiny tubers packed with nutrients. Rich in fibre, healthy fats, vitamins E and C, and natural sugars — they've been consumed in Nigeria for centuries both as a snack and for making the beloved tigernut milk drink. Our tigernuts are carefully harvested, cleaned, and dried to preserve their full nutritional profile.

Rich in dietary fibre — supports digestive health
High in magnesium, potassium, and iron
Natural prebiotic — feeds healthy gut bacteria
Gluten-free and dairy-free — suitable for all diets
Available dried, fresh, or as tigernut flour

Natural Sweetener & Superfruit

Dates
Dabino

Nature's perfect sweetener — energy-dense, deeply nutritious, wonderfully sweet

Known as Dabino in Hausa-speaking Nigeria, dates are one of the world's most ancient and revered fruits. Our premium dates are sourced from the best suppliers — plump, naturally sweet, and packed with essential minerals. Used as a natural sweetener, snack, or gift, they are a staple of Nigerian healthy living and a perfect alternative to refined sugar in cooking and baking.

Excellent natural source of quick energy
High in potassium, magnesium, and iron
Natural sweetener — perfect for diabetic-friendly cooking
Rich in antioxidants — Medjool and Sukkari varieties available
Ideal for gifting, smoothies, and baking
₦2,500 / 1kg box

Dried Hibiscus Flowers

Hibiscus
Zobo Leaves

Nigeria's beloved ruby-red drink — bold, tangy, and bursting with health benefits

Zobo is arguably Nigeria's most iconic homemade drink — a deeply coloured, tangy, refreshing beverage brewed from dried hibiscus flowers. Our premium zobo leaves are carefully dried, cleaned, and packed to preserve their natural colour, aroma, and the full spectrum of their health-giving compounds. Whether for drinking, cooking, or export, PRP zobo leaves are the finest grade available.

Rich in antioxidants — anthocyanins and flavonoids
Supports healthy blood pressure naturally
High in vitamin C — natural immune booster
Vibrant deep-red colour — excellent for natural food dye
Available in small retail packs and large wholesale bags
